# 01 — Concept

## Elevator pitch

GRINDSET is a top-down, browser-native MMORPG where every click, swing, and smelted bar feeds a single on-chain currency. It wears its grind on its sleeve — the game is self-aware about being a digital skinner box, and that honesty *is* the brand.

## Core fantasy

You're a washed-up nobody who lands in Mireholm — a fantasy world whose economy collapsed when the ancient Council of Scribes (the devs) abandoned it. Adventurers now run the show. You grind skills, carve out a niche, and try to get rich before the next rugpull god wakes up.

## Core loop (60 seconds)

1. Pick a skill node (tree, rock, fish, mob).
2. Tick-based interaction resolves (400ms server tick).
3. XP + loot + `$GRIND` drip in.
4. Spend on gear, levels, territory, consumables — most of which burn or sink the token.
5. Level unlocks better nodes, better regions, better loot tables. Repeat at higher tier.

## What makes it different from OSRS

- Faster tick (400ms vs 600ms), snappier animations, modern UI.
- Mobile-capable from day 1.
- The gold piece is a real, tradeable SPL token with a price chart.
- Rare items are optionally NFT-mintable at player cost.
- Seasons: every ~4 months, a new region + skill tier unlocks. Top of leaderboard wins treasury slice.
- Short XP ladder (level 20 per skill) — drama at every level, full kit reachable in one season.

## Target feel

*OSRS meets a degen Discord server.* You log in to grind, you stay for the banter and the price action.

## Constraints

- Original IP — inspired by, not copied from, RuneScape.
- Buildable by a small team (~8 FTE).
- No pay-to-win.
- No VC allocation in token.
- No mandatory KYC to play.
